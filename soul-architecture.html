CHAPTER · IX

The Final Transcendence

You are not ultimately explained by your intelligence, your achievements, your failures, or even your self-understanding.

You are, first and foremost, known by the One who made you.

The chief end of humanity, as the old confession puts it, is "to glorify God, and to enjoy Him forever." In that vision, purpose is not an algorithm waiting to be cracked. It unfolds through faithful living — loving God, loving neighbor, cultivating the gifts you've been given, and trusting that the Author of the story sees the whole architecture even when you only see a single room.
